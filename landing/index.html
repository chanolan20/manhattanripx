Solve the real problems

Every money leak in your
DTF shop, plugged.

Most DTF shops lose margin in four places: ink waste, reprints, slow nesting, and expensive RIP licenses. Manhattan RIP X attacks all four from day one.

Gang Sheet Builder
Printing one design per sheet is burning your film and ink budget. The auto-nester packs every job tight — drag-and-drop or fully automatic — so you never waste a centimeter of film.
AI Auto-Profiler
Generic ICC profiles cost you ink and reprints. Print our test chart once, scan it, and get a color profile built for your exact ink batch, film, and powder — usually in under 3 minutes.
Hot Folders + Shopify
Stop copy-pasting orders into your queue. Drop files in a watched folder or connect your Shopify store — orders auto-import, auto-nest into gang sheets, and queue for print while you're doing something else.
Separation Studio
Blown-out whites and muddy colors on dark shirts come from a bad white underbase. Separation Studio gives you CMYK+W channel control, spot color targeting, and halftone settings so the white hits hard and the colors sit on top of it clean.
Image Tools (Built-in)
Customers send you 72 dpi screenshots and backgrounds they forgot to remove. Fix resolution, strip backgrounds, generate halftones, and knock out colors without switching to Photoshop or paying for another subscription.
Real-Time RIP Progress
Know exactly where each job is — not just a spinning wheel. Per-job progress streams live so you can sequence your pulls, heat press timing, and batch hand-off without standing over the machine.
21+ ET-8550 Print Modes
Factory-tuned modes for every scenario: color opaque, forever dark, no-raster DTG simulation, high detail, ultra fine — all pre-calibrated for the ET-8550's 7-channel ink system so you're not guessing settings.
Per-Job Color Adjust
Your master profile is for most jobs. But some artwork needs a nudge. CMYK sliders, brightness, saturation, and ink removal controls let you dial in a single job without touching your global settings or reprinting.
Multi-Queue Management
Run Production and Proofing queues side by side — different media, different profiles, different priorities. Rush orders jump the queue. Proofs never accidentally go to the main printer.
